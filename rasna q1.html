Sports Week

Sports Week is a special event organized in schools, colleges, and communities to promote physical fitness, teamwork, and a healthy competitive spirit. It usually spans several days and includes a variety of activities such as athletics, team games, and fun recreational events.

The main objectives of Sports Week are to encourage students to participate in physical activities, develop sportsmanship, and strengthen bonds among peers. It also provides a platform for discovering and nurturing talent in different sports. Beyond competition, it fosters discipline, perseverance, and a sense of unity, making it an important part of holistic education.
